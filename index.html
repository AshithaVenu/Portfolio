01 / ABOUT

My work sits at the intersection of remote sensing, machine learning, and environmental monitoring. Using optical and microwave satellite data — Sentinel, Landsat, and radar missions, I build models that classify land cover, estimate biomass, and predict environmental risk in places where ground data is scarce. Each project below started as a research question and ended as a reproducible pipeline, published openly on GitHub.
